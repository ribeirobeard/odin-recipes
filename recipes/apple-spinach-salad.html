Apple Spinach Salad

The addition of nuts and fruit makes this salad extra special.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups baby spinach leaves
  • 1 medium apple, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons chopped celery
  • 2 tablespoons toasted PLANTERS Pecans, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons KRAFT LIGHT DONE RIGHT!

Steps

  1. Toss ingredients with dressing in large bowl.
